Our Signature Dishes

Sizzling Lamb Kulma

A Tausug-inspired lamb stew with rich coconut milk, curry, peanut butter, and spices. Served sizzling hot with a unique Mindanaoan flair.

Chicken Piyanggang

A Maranao dish made with chicken simmered in coconut milk and burnt coconut paste, giving it a deep black color and smoky flavor.

Beef Rendang

Slow-cooked beef in aromatic spices and coconut milk. This hearty dish is both tender and packed with flavor.

Sinuglaw

A perfect blend of grilled pork (sinugba) and fresh fish ceviche (kinilaw), marinated in vinegar, ginger, and chili for a zesty kick.
