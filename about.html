Welcome to Cucina Higala 🍽️

Cucina Higala isn’t just a restaurant—it’s an immersive culinary experience. Our mission is to treat every higala (friend) to a flavorful journey inspired by the heart and soul of Mindanao. 🌴

We specialize in traditional Filipino dishes, proudly showcasing our beloved Mindanao Heritage Dishes. From local classics to heirloom recipes, every bite is a tribute to the region’s rich cultural tapestry and vibrant flavors.

A Celebration of Culture Through Cuisine

Each dish tells a story—from ancestral recipes passed down through generations to the unique spices of Mindanao’s diverse communities. We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients to bring these stories to life—supporting local farmers and celebrating homegrown goodness.

Our menu is a mosaic of Mindanao’s culinary identities, creatively presented to honor indigenous traditions while appealing to modern tastes. It’s food that connects, heals, and celebrates heritage.

More Than Just Dining

Cucina Higala is a place to gather, laugh, and make memories. Whether you're dining with family, friends, or visiting for the first time, you'll feel right at home in our cozy space—filled with warm lighting, wooden textures, and a touch of nostalgia that reflects our love for Filipino culture.

Our décor and ambiance are thoughtfully curated to transport you back to a simpler time—where stories were shared over meals, and every visit felt like coming home.

Experience the Best of Mindanao

Join us on a flavorful journey through the soul of the South. Discover why we’re one of Cagayan de Oro’s most beloved dining destinations. At Cucina Higala, every meal is a celebration, and every guest is family. 💖
